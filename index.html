Our Story

We built this
because of Maddie.

Madisen Ruth Lutz was Bradley's oldest sister. When she died suddenly and far too young in May 2026, none of us were ready. No one really is. But we had no idea that the hardest part wasn't going to be the grief itself. It was everything that came after.

In the middle of our loss, we found ourselves facing questions we had no way to answer. Where was her life insurance? Who were her beneficiaries? What did she want for her service? We didn't know. Not because Maddie was careless. She wasn't. Because no one had ever sat down with her and made it simple. Watching her parents work through the weight of settling her estate on top of their grief was something we will never forget.

"Settling her estate was almost as hard as losing her.
It should never be that way."

I'm Beza. I have been working as a licensed insurance broker for over five years. I know exactly where families get stuck: the 401k with an ex-spouse still listed, the policy nobody can locate, the account that goes into probate because a form was never updated. I had seen all of it professionally for years. Then I lived it in my own family. Bradley is a software engineer. He builds systems that solve real problems. We looked at each other and said the same thing: this cannot keep happening to people, and we have everything it takes to fix it.

Legaci Reserve™ is our answer to what we went through. Every session we do is the session we wish someone had done for Maddie. Every binder that ships is one less family left searching in the middle of the worst days of their lives. This is personal for us every single time. That is exactly why we do it this way.
